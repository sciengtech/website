Custom Design & Turnkey Solutions: Tailored development of regenerative cavity-like laser delay lines for advanced pump–probe experiments

Long Delay Capability: Enables probe pulse delay of up to 1 μs, overcoming limitations of conventional free-space delay lines

Efficient Pulse Utilization: Laser pulse is split, with ~10% of the beam (~700 μJ) used as the probe

Supercontinuum Generation: Probe pulse is used to generate supercontinuum in photonic crystal fibers, requiring ~100 μJ per pulse

Compact Alternative to Long Optical Paths: Avoids impractical ~300 m optical path length required for mirror-based delay systems
